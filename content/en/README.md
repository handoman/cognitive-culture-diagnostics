# English Content

The core monograph of this project is written in Swedish and is grounded in Swedish clinical practice and diagnostic context.

The author does not plan to produce an official English translation of the monograph, as this would require linguistic and clinical competencies beyond the scope of this project.

However, the material is licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0.  
Forking, translating, and adapting the work into other languages is explicitly permitted under the terms of the license.

Contributions in the form of translations or adaptations are welcome via fork and pull request, provided they respect the original structure, scope, and attribution requirements.
