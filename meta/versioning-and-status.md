# Versioning and Project Status

This document tracks the **structural and developmental status** of the Cognitive Culture Diagnostics project.

Its purpose is to distinguish between:
- structural decisions,
- content development,
- and future revisions.

It is not a detailed change log.

---

## Versioning Philosophy

Versioning in this project follows a **semantic distinction** between:

- **Structural changes**  
  Changes that affect the architecture, scope, or conceptual organization of the monograph.

- **Content changes**  
  Refinements, expansions, or clarifications within an already defined structure.

Structural changes are rare and explicitly documented.  
Content changes are expected and ongoing.

## Change Documentation

Any change that affects:
- chapter order,
- conceptual scope,
- methodological orientation,
- or terminology policy

should be documented here with:
- date,
- brief description,
- rationale.

Minor edits do not require documentation.

---

## Stability Statement

The project is currently in a **structurally stable phase**.

Future work is expected to:
- extend research,
- test hypotheses,
- or develop tools,

rather than revise the foundational architecture.
